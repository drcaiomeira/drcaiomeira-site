Online consultation

How to take and send standardized photos

High-quality photos significantly improve evaluation—especially for revision and structural cases. Follow the steps below and send them via WhatsApp.

Lighting

  • Natural light from the front (near a window)
  • Avoid overhead lighting (creates shadows)
  • Avoid strong flash and filters

Background & position

  • Plain background (light wall)
  • Camera at nose level
  • Hair pulled back, face fully visible

Settings

  • Avoid “portrait mode” (can distort)
  • Avoid extreme digital zoom
  • Distance ~ 1 meter

Required angles (6 photos)

  • Front (straight ahead)
  • Right profile
  • Left profile
  • Right 3/4 (oblique)
  • Left 3/4
  • Base view (from below)

If possible, include one photo smiling and one at rest with lips gently closed.

Tips to reduce distortion

  • Use the rear camera (better quality)
  • Do not hold the phone too close
  • If possible, ask someone to take the photos
  • Keep head neutral (no tilt)
Send photos on WhatsApp

Avoid filters/edits. Sharp, well-lit photos are essential.

What to send along with the photos

  • Age and city/country
  • Main goal (natural, male, revision, augmentation, etc.)
  • If previously operated: year of surgery and current concerns
  • If available: reports, exams, trauma history
